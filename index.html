Graduate Education

Pioneering the urban and regional planning education in the Philippines, UP SURP offers innovative degree programs that cater to the students' varying academic aspirations.

What sets UP SURP apart from its contemporaries is the delivery of specialized courses, allowing aspiring planners to direct their passion and contribution to specific planning sectors.

Training and Extension Services

Fulfilling its training and extension mandates, the School maintains a working relationship with many government inter-agency committees. legislative bodies, and local governments, and assists them with their planning and capacity building needs.

Research and Publication

UP SURP remains responsive to its research mandate through independent and collaborative studies that are not only geared towards knowledge-generation, but also translate into practice and policy-making.

SURP Library

The school library was established in 1968. The library provides a wide array of learning resources to support the School’s functions of conducting academic programs in planning; providing training courses, seminars, workshops in cooperation with other government agencies in carrying out research activities designed to solve problems of development particularly on settlement patterns and environmental studies.

Recent

Events


UP SURP participates in the Philippine Stakeholder Forum in Housing and Urban Development Management

On 25 April 2025, the UP SURP Dean Dina C. Magnaye joined the United Nations Human Settlements Programme (UNHSP) in a forum discussion on housing and urban development management. UN Undersecretary General and UN Habitat Executive Director Anaclaudia Rossbach shared insights on the key issues as reflected in the UN Habitat’s New Strategic Plan 2026-2029, proposing possible ways forward, as well as collaborative opportunities.

UP SURP Participated in the "Hands-on Sustainable Urban Mobility Program" held in Karlsruhe, Germany

Delegates from the UP SURP participated in the “Hands-on Sustainable Urban Mobility Program,” held from April 6 to 12, 2025, hosted by Hochschule Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences (HKA) in Germany. The program brought together urban planners and mobility experts internationally to participate in activities and scholarly discussions on sustainable and inclusive transport systems.

UP SURP Participates in the Nationwide Earthquake Drill

On 13 March 2025, the UP SURP community participated in the Nationwide Earthquake Drill, aiming to assess and improve upon the immediate evacuation and response of the school in the face of disasters.

DILG MIMAROPA and UP SURP Reinforce Commitment to Strengthen the Local Development Planning Capacity of MIMAROPA LGUs

True to its commitment in building capable professional urban and regional planners in the country, the School strengthens its partnership with government institutions in delivering innovative and relevant graduate courses, training, research, and extension services.

UP SURP and Commission on Population and Development explore research and capacity-building opportunities

On 13 February 2025, the UP SURP had an exploratory meeting with the Office of the Executive Director of the Commission on Population and Development (CPD-OED) to discuss entry points for future collaboration.
